# High Value Customers Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers who contribute the highest revenue and place the most orders.

## Objective

Analyze customer value and identify the most important customers for retention and growth strategies.

## SQL Concepts Used

* INNER JOIN
* GROUP BY
* COUNT()
* SUM()
* ORDER BY

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Joined customers and orders tables.
2. Calculated total orders placed by each customer.
3. Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.
4. Ranked customers based on revenue contribution.

## Findings

1. A small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue.
2. High-value customers tend to place multiple orders.
3. Customer retention is critical for maintaining revenue growth.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create VIP customer programs.
2. Offer personalized promotions to top customers.
3. Monitor high-value customers to reduce churn risk.
