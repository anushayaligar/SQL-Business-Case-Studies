
# Cross Category Customer Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers who purchase products across the largest number of product categories.

## Objective

Analyze customer purchasing behavior to identify highly engaged customers who shop across multiple product categories.

## SQL Concepts Used

- INNER JOIN
- COUNT(DISTINCT)
- GROUP BY
- ORDER BY
- Multi-Table Joins

## Tables Used

- customers
- orders
- order_items
- products

## Analysis Approach

1. Connected customers to their orders.
2. Linked orders to purchased products.
3. Counted the number of unique categories purchased by each customer.
4. Ranked customers based on category diversity.

## Findings

1. Some customers purchase across multiple categories.
2. Cross-category customers are highly engaged with the business.
3. Customers buying from multiple categories are likely to have higher lifetime value.

## Business Recommendations

1. Target cross-category customers with loyalty programs.
2. Recommend complementary products from additional categories.
3. Use these customers as a benchmark for customer engagement strategies.
