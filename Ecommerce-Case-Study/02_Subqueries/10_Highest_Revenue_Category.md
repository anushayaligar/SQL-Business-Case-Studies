
# Highest Revenue Category Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify the product category generating the highest total revenue.

## Objective

Determine the most profitable category and support strategic business decisions.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Subqueries
* Derived Tables
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* MAX()
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated revenue for each category.
2. Determined the maximum category revenue.
3. Returned the category generating the highest revenue.
4. Identified the strongest-performing business segment.

## Findings

1. Revenue is concentrated within specific product categories.
2. The top category contributes significantly to overall business performance.
3. Category-level analysis helps prioritize business investments.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory and marketing for top-performing categories.
2. Analyze successful category characteristics and apply them elsewhere.
3. Monitor category performance regularly to identify trends.

* Category Analytics
* Revenue Analysis
* SQL Subqueries
