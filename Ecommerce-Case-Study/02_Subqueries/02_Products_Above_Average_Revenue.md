
# Products Above Average Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify products generating revenue higher than the average product revenue.

## Objective

Determine which products outperform the average revenue benchmark and contribute significantly to overall business revenue.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Subqueries
* Derived Tables
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* AVG()
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated revenue for each product using quantity × price.
2. Calculated average revenue across all products.
3. Compared each product's revenue against the average product revenue.
4. Identified products performing above the benchmark.

## Findings

1. Certain products generate substantially more revenue than the average product.
2. High-revenue products contribute disproportionately to overall business performance.
3. Revenue generation is concentrated among a small number of products.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase marketing efforts for top-performing products.
2. Maintain adequate inventory levels for high-revenue products.
3. Analyze factors contributing to the success of these products and apply learnings to other products.
