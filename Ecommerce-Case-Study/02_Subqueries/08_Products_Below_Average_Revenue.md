# Products Below Average Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify products generating revenue below the average product revenue.

## Objective

Find underperforming products that contribute less revenue than the average product.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Subqueries
* Derived Tables
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* AVG()
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated revenue for each product using quantity × price.
2. Calculated average revenue across all products.
3. Compared each product's revenue against the average.
4. Identified products generating below-average revenue.

## Findings

1. Some products generate significantly less revenue than the average product.
2. Revenue contribution is uneven across the product portfolio.
3. Low-revenue products may indicate weak demand or ineffective product positioning.

## Business Recommendations

1. Review pricing strategies for underperforming products.
2. Increase product visibility through targeted promotions.
3. Evaluate whether low-performing products should be improved, repositioned, or discontinued.
