# Products Above Average Quantity Sold Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify products that sold more units than the average product.

## Objective

Find products with above-average customer demand.

## SQL Concepts Used

- Subqueries
- Derived Tables
- GROUP BY
- HAVING
- AVG()
- SUM()

## Tables Used

- products
- order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated total quantity sold for each product.
2. Calculated average quantity sold across all products.
3. Compared each product's sales quantity against the average.
4. Identified products with above-average demand.

## Findings

1. Certain products have significantly higher sales volume than average.
2. Product demand is concentrated among a few products.
3. High-demand products drive a large share of unit sales.

## Business Recommendations

1. Maintain higher inventory levels for high-demand products.
2. Increase visibility of best-selling products.
3. Analyze customer preferences for top-performing products.
