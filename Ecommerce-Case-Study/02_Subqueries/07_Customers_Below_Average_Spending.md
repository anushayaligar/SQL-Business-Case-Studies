# Customers Below Average Spending Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers whose spending is below the average customer spending.

## Objective

Find low-engagement customers who may require targeted marketing efforts.

## SQL Concepts Used

- Subqueries
- Derived Tables
- GROUP BY
- HAVING
- AVG()
- SUM()

## Tables Used

- customers
- orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated total spending for each customer.
2. Calculated average customer spending.
3. Compared customer spending against the average.
4. Identified customers below the benchmark.

## Findings

1. Some customers contribute less revenue than the average customer.
2. These customers represent an opportunity for growth.
3. Targeted engagement strategies may increase spending.

## Business Recommendations

1. Offer personalized promotions.
2. Encourage repeat purchases through loyalty programs.
3. Analyze purchasing behavior to identify barriers to spending.
