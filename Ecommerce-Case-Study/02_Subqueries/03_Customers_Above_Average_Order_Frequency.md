
# Customers Above Average Order Frequency Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers who place more orders than the average customer.

## Objective

Find highly engaged customers based on purchase frequency.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Subqueries
* Derived Tables
* GROUP BY
* HAVING
* AVG()
* COUNT()

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated the number of orders placed by each customer.
2. Calculated the average order count across all customers.
3. Compared each customer's order count against the average.
4. Identified customers with above-average purchasing frequency.

## Findings

1. A small group of customers places orders more frequently than average.
2. Repeat customers contribute significantly to business activity.
3. High-frequency customers are valuable for retention strategies.

## Business Recommendations

1. Introduce loyalty rewards for repeat customers.
2. Create personalized offers for frequent buyers.
3. Monitor engagement patterns to improve customer retention.
