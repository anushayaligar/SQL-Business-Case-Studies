# Products Above Average Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify products generating revenue higher than the average product revenue.

## Objective

Find high-performing products that contribute more revenue than the average product and support inventory and marketing decisions.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Multiple CTEs
* INNER JOIN
* CROSS JOIN
* AVG()
* SUM()
* ORDER BY

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate revenue generated by each product.
2. Created a second CTE to calculate average product revenue.
3. Compared each product's revenue against the average revenue benchmark.
4. Identified products generating above-average revenue.

## Findings

1. A subset of products generates significantly higher revenue than the average product.
2. Revenue is concentrated among a few top-performing products.
3. High-revenue products are key drivers of business growth.

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize inventory planning for high-performing products.
2. Increase marketing efforts for products generating strong revenue.
3. Analyze successful product characteristics and apply learnings to other products.
