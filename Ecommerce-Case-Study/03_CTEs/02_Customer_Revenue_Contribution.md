
# Customer Revenue Contribution Analysis

## Business Problem

Calculate each customer's contribution to the company's total revenue.

## Objective

Identify how much revenue each customer contributes to overall business performance and determine the most valuable customers.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Multiple CTEs
* CROSS JOIN
* SUM()
* ROUND()
* Percentage Calculations

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate total revenue generated by each customer.
2. Created another CTE to calculate total company revenue.
3. Joined customer revenue with customer details.
4. Calculated each customer's revenue contribution percentage.
5. Ranked customers by revenue contribution.

## Findings

1. A small group of customers contributes a large percentage of total company revenue.
2. Revenue generation is not evenly distributed across all customers.
3. High-value customers have a significant impact on overall business performance.

## Business Recommendations

1. Develop VIP programs for top revenue contributors.
2. Implement retention strategies for high-value customers.
3. Analyze purchasing behavior of top customers to identify growth opportunities.
