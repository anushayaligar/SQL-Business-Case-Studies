# Top 5 Products By Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify the top 5 products generating the highest revenue.

## Objective

Determine the highest-performing products and understand which products contribute most to business revenue.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* INNER JOIN
* GROUP BY
* SUM()
* ORDER BY
* LIMIT

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate revenue generated by each product.
2. Joined products and order_items tables using product_id.
3. Calculated revenue using quantity × price.
4. Ranked products by revenue in descending order.
5. Selected the top 5 revenue-generating products.

## Findings

1. A small group of products contributes a large share of total revenue.
2. High-performing products play a critical role in business growth.
3. Product performance varies significantly across the catalog.

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize inventory management for top-performing products.
2. Increase marketing efforts for high-revenue products.
3. Analyze successful product characteristics and apply insights to other products.
