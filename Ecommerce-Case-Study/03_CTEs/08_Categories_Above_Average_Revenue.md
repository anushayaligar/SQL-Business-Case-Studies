# Categories Above Average Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify product categories generating revenue above the average category revenue.

## Objective

Determine high-performing categories that contribute more revenue than the average category and support strategic business decisions.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Multiple CTEs
* INNER JOIN
* CROSS JOIN
* GROUP BY
* AVG()
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate revenue generated by each category.
2. Created a second CTE to calculate average category revenue.
3. Compared each category's revenue against the average revenue benchmark.
4. Identified categories performing above average.

## Findings

1. Certain categories significantly outperform the average category revenue.
2. Revenue generation is concentrated among a small number of categories.
3. High-performing categories play a major role in overall business success.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory investment in high-performing categories.
2. Allocate additional marketing budget to profitable categories.
3. Analyze successful category characteristics and replicate them across other categories.
