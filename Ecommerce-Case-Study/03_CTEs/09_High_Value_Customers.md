
# High Value Customers Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers contributing more than 20% of the total company revenue.

## Objective

Find high-value customers whose revenue contribution has a significant impact on overall business performance.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Multiple CTEs
* INNER JOIN
* CROSS JOIN
* SUM()
* ROUND()
* WHERE Clause

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.
2. Calculated total company revenue.
3. Computed each customer's percentage contribution to total revenue.
4. Filtered customers contributing more than 20% of overall revenue.

## Findings

1. A small number of customers contribute a large share of total revenue.
2. Business revenue is concentrated among key customers.
3. Losing these customers could significantly impact company performance.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create VIP retention programs for high-value customers.
2. Offer personalized discounts and loyalty rewards.
3. Monitor purchasing behavior and customer satisfaction closely.
