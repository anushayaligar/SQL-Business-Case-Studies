# Category Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify the total revenue generated by each product category.

## Objective

Analyze category-level performance and determine which categories contribute the most revenue to the business.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expression (CTE)
* INNER JOIN
* GROUP BY
* SUM()
* ORDER BY

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate revenue for each category.
2. Joined products and order_items tables using product_id.
3. Calculated category revenue using quantity × price.
4. Ranked categories based on total revenue generated.

## Findings

1. Revenue is concentrated among a few product categories.
2. Top-performing categories contribute significantly to overall sales.
3. Category-level analysis helps identify business strengths.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory investment in top-performing categories.
2. Allocate more marketing budget to high-revenue categories.
3. Analyze low-performing categories for improvement opportunities.
