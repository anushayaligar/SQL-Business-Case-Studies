# Customers Above Average Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify customers whose total revenue contribution is above the average customer revenue.

## Objective

Find high-value customers for loyalty programs, retention campaigns, and targeted marketing.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Multiple CTEs
* INNER JOIN
* CROSS JOIN
* AVG()
* SUM()
* ORDER BY

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate revenue generated by each customer.
2. Created a second CTE to calculate average customer revenue.
3. Joined customer information with revenue data.
4. Identified customers whose revenue exceeded the average benchmark.

## Findings

1. A subset of customers generates significantly higher revenue than average.
2. High-value customers contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
3. Revenue distribution is concentrated among a limited customer segment.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create VIP programs for high-value customers.
2. Offer personalized promotions and rewards.
3. Monitor customer retention and engagement for top contributors.
