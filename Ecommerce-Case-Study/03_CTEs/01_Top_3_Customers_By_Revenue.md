
# Customer Revenue Contribution Analysis

## Business Problem

Determine the percentage contribution of each customer to the company's total revenue.

## Objective

Identify the customers contributing the highest share of revenue and understand revenue concentration across the customer base.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Aggregations
* SUM()
* Calculated Metrics
* Percentage Calculations

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Created a CTE to calculate total revenue generated by each customer.
2. Calculated total company revenue using the CTE.
3. Computed each customer's percentage contribution to total revenue.
4. Ranked customers based on revenue contribution.

## Findings

1. A small number of customers contribute a significant percentage of overall revenue.
2. Revenue distribution is not uniform across customers.
3. High-contributing customers have a major impact on business performance.

## Business Recommendations

1. Develop VIP programs for top revenue contributors.
2. Focus retention efforts on high-value customers.
3. Monitor revenue concentration to reduce dependency on a small customer segment.
