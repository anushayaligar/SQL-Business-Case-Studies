
# Customer Spending Trend Analysis

## Business Problem

Determine whether customer spending increased, decreased, or remained stable compared to previous purchases.

## Objective

Identify customer behavior trends and potential churn risks.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Window Functions
* LAG()
* CASE WHEN
* PARTITION BY
* ORDER BY
* CTE

## Tables Used

* orders

## Findings

1. Spending trends reveal customer engagement levels.
2. Declining spending may indicate reduced customer interest.
3. Increasing spending highlights growth opportunities.

## Business Recommendations

1. Target customers with decreasing spending using retention campaigns.
2. Reward customers with increasing spending.
3. Monitor trend changes regularly.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Customer Behavior Analysis
* Trend Analysis
* Window Functions
* Business Intelligence
