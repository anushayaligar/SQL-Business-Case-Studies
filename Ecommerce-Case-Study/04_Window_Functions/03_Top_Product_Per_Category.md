
# Top Product Per Category Analysis

## Business Problem

Identify the highest revenue-generating product in each category.

## Objective

Determine category leaders and understand which products drive category performance.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Window Functions
* ROW_NUMBER()
* PARTITION BY
* ORDER BY
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated revenue generated by each product.
2. Ranked products within each category using ROW_NUMBER().
3. Selected the top-ranked product from every category.
4. Identified category leaders based on revenue.

## Findings

1. Every category has a clear top-performing product.
2. Category leaders contribute a significant portion of category revenue.
3. Top products are critical for inventory and marketing decisions.

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize stock availability for category-leading products.
2. Increase marketing efforts around top-performing products.
3. Analyze successful product characteristics and replicate them across other products.
