# Customer Revenue Percentage Analysis

## Business Problem

Calculate each customer's contribution to total company revenue.

## Objective

Identify customers who contribute the most revenue to the business.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Window Functions
* SUM() OVER()
* ROUND()
* CTE

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Findings

1. Revenue contribution is concentrated among key customers.
2. High-value customers significantly impact business performance.
3. Revenue concentration risk can be identified.

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize retention of high-contributing customers.
2. Create VIP customer programs.
3. Diversify revenue sources to reduce dependency on a few customers.

## Skills Demonstrated

* Revenue Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Window Functions
* Business Intelligence
