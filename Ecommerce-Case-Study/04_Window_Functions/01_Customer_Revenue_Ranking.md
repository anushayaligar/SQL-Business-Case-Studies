# Customer Revenue Ranking Analysis

## Business Problem

Rank customers based on the total revenue they generate for the company.

## Objective

Identify top-performing customers and understand revenue distribution across the customer base.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Window Functions
* RANK()
* ORDER BY
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.
2. Applied RANK() to rank customers by revenue.
3. Assigned rankings from highest to lowest revenue.

## Findings

1. Top-ranked customers contribute a significant share of total revenue.
2. Multiple customers can share the same rank when revenue values are equal.
3. Customer ranking helps prioritize retention efforts.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create loyalty programs for top-ranked customers.
2. Monitor changes in customer rankings over time.
3. Develop personalized engagement strategies for high-value customers.
