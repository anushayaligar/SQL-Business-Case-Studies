
# Customer Segmentation Analysis

## Business Problem

Segment customers into groups based on revenue contribution.

## Objective

Categorize customers into different value segments for targeted business strategies.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Window Functions
* NTILE()
* ORDER BY
* CTE

## Tables Used

* customers
* orders

## Findings

1. Customers can be grouped into high, medium, and low-value segments.
2. Revenue contribution varies significantly across segments.
3. Segmentation improves customer targeting.

## Business Recommendations

1. Create VIP programs for top segments.
2. Upsell medium-value customers.
3. Increase engagement efforts for lower-value segments.

* Customer Segmentation
* Revenue Analysis
* Window Functions
