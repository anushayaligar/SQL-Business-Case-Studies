
# Product Ranking Within Category Analysis

## Business Problem

Rank products by revenue within each category.

## Objective

Identify the best-performing products inside each product category.

## SQL Concepts Used

* Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
* Window Functions
* RANK()
* PARTITION BY
* ORDER BY
* SUM()

## Tables Used

* products
* order_items

## Analysis Approach

1. Calculated revenue generated by each product.
2. Partitioned products by category.
3. Ranked products within each category based on revenue.

## Findings

1. Product performance varies significantly within categories.
2. Some products dominate category revenue contribution.
3. Category-level rankings highlight product leaders.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory for top-ranked products.
2. Promote high-performing products within each category.
3. Analyze low-performing products for improvement opportunities.

* Product Analytics
* Category Analysis
* Window Functions
* Ranking Analysis
